Step Description
Mockup Needed? ($500 Fee: Desktop/Mobile Mockup + 1 Round of Revisions)
  • PFM works with Design to complete mockup and gains dealer approval.
  • PFM gains approval from T2/Content for mockup.
PFM Creates Salesforce Case
  • Subject line must include "Custom Design Request".
  • Link Project Outline document, client mockup, and all project info.
  • Mark diagnosis as “Project” and pass to "Design" queue.
Design Queue
  • Design assigns Designer and communicates Design Due Date.
  • Designer uses “Mockup Approval” workflow in #support-custom-project channel.
  • Development team reviews mockup for platform compatibility.
  • Designer posts client-ready mockup in Support Ticket and passes to PFM/Requestor.
PFM Review
  • PFM reviews mockup with client.
  • If revisions are needed, go back to Step 1a.
  • If client approves, close ticket and move to next step (CPQ, new support ticket).
CPQ Opportunity & Support Case
  • PFM creates CPQ Opportunity for custom work and ensures it's ready to be billed (include design and custom work fees).
  • Create Salesforce case with "Custom Development Request" in subject.
  • Link Project Outline, approved CPQ Opportunity, mockup, and all text/images needed.
  • Pass case to appropriate team (Tier 2/Content).
  • CC team supervisor (Content: Khrissy Gadomski, Tier 2: Amanda Hershey/Mike Sanchez).
Completion and Approval
  • Appropriate team completes changes and provides estimated completion date.
  • Team shares complete work or dev site for approval.
  • Approval is final; no revisions after quote is paid.
  • Additional revisions will be billed at $125/hour with prior agreement.
  • Service Start Date (SSD) must be added to CPQ Opportunity to activate billing.
  • Once SSD is added, project is completed and no further adjustments can be made.
